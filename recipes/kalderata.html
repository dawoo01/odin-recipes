Beef Kaldereta

Description

Beef Kaldereta is a main stay in any Filipino Kitchen. It is a type of beef stew cooked with tomato sauce and liver spread. Ingredients such as potato, carrots, bell peppers, and olives are also utilized when cooking this dish.

This is a popular dish is served during special occasions. It is a regular item in every Filipino cookbook. Some consider kaldereta as the Philippines most famous beef stew.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef cubed
  • 3 pieces garlic cloves crushed and chopped
  • 1 piece onion finely chopped
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 piece red bell pepper sliced
  • 1 piece green bell pepper sliced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • ½ cup liver spread processed using blender
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 2 cups potatoes sliced
  • 2 cups carrots sliced
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • ⅔ cup green olives
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the cooking oil in the cooking pot or pressure cooker. 1/4 cup cooking oil
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic. 1 piece onion, 3 pieces garlic cloves
  3. Add the beef. Cook for 5 minutes or until the color turns light brown. 2 lbs beef
  4. Add the dried bay leaves and chili flakes or crushed pepper. Stir. 3 pieces dried bay leaves, 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  5. Add the liver spread. Stir. ½ cup liver spread
  6. Pour-in the tomato sauce and beef broth. 1 cup tomato sauce, 2 cups beef broth
  7. Cook the beef until it becomes tender (about 30 mins if using a pressure cooker, or 1 to 2 hours if using an ordinary pot).
  8. Add potato and carrots. Cook for 8 to 10 minutes. 2 cups potatoes, 2 cups carrots
  9. Put the green olives and bell peppers in the cooking pot. Stir and continue to cook for 5 minutes more. 1 piece red bell pepper, ⅔ cup green olives, 1 piece green bell pepper
  10. Add salt and pepper to taste salt and pepper to taste
  11. Serve Hot. Enjoy!
